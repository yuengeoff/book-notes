## Chapter 10: How to Find and Fix the Causes of Your Bad Habits
* pg 130: Habits are attractive when we associate them with positive feelings. 
* pg 132: Motivation Ritual: the act of associating your habits with something you enjoy

## Chapter 12: The Law of Least Effort
* pg 153: Habits are easier to build when they fit into the flow of your life. You are most likely to go to the gym if it is on your way to work because stopping doesn't add much friction to your lifestyle. 
* pg 156: Prime the environment for future use 
> Whenever he takes a shower, he wipes down the toilet while the shower is warming up. 
> 
> Set out your workout clothes, shoes, gym bag, and water bottle ahead of time. 
> 
> Leave your phone in a different room until lunch.
